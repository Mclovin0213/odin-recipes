Homemade Pepporoni Pizza

My kids love pizza, so pepperoni pizza is usually a surefire dinner win. But if I'm
honest, I rarely make pizza for my kids. I view pizza as a cheat night for me, and I get delivery.

So when I decided to make pizza at home, I knew it needed to be better than your
average delivery. It needed tons of pepperoni, a nice crust, great flavor,
and maybe even a surprise or two to put it over the top.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
  7. Make the honey butter (optional)
